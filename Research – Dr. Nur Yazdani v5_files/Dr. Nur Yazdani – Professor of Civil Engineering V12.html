Dr. Nur Yazdani is a distinguished Professor and former Chairperson of the Civil Engineering Department at UT Arlington. Recognized as a Fellow of ASCE, ACI, and SEI, he has contributed significantly to the field with over 180 publications in reputable journals and conference proceedings. Renowned for his expertise, Dr. Yazdani has been invited to share his insights at conferences and seminars worldwide.

His research spans various critical areas including Concrete Structures Design, Evaluation, and Rehabilitation, Sustainable Technologies for the Built Environment, Resilient Infrastructure, NDE applications, Concrete Properties, Coastal Infrastructure, Building Codes, and Engineering Education. Dr. Yazdani's dedication and excellence have earned him numerous awards for both teaching and research. Furthermore, his innovative approach has attracted over $17.5 million in funding for externally-funded research projects, furthering the advancement of civil engineering knowledge and practice.

Background

Dr. Nur Yazdani has been very active in national societies, such as the ASCE, ACI and the TRB. He is a past president of the ASCE Tallahassee Branch, past Chairman of the ACI-SEI Joint Committee 343 on Concrete Bridge Design and current Chairman of the SEI Fort Worth Branch. Dr. Yazdani has organized many technical sessions at national/international conferences. He is an ASCE certified ABET program evaluator.

Funding sources have included a wide variety of federal, state and local sources, such as the National Science Foundation (NSF), Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA), US Department of Transportation (USDOT), Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) and Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT). Research results have been widely adopted and practiced by state and federal agencies. Leadership roles have included the Chair of the ACI-SEI Committee 343 on Concrete Bridge Design, SEI Committee on Retrofit of Structures for Dynamic Loads, SEI Membership Committee and SEI Fort Worth Branch. Dr. Yazdani is a senior ASCE certified ABET civil engineering program evaluator and team chair. He conceived and leads the Disaster Mitigation Group and an NDE Laboratory at UT Arlington. Dr. Yazdani serves or served on the editorial board of ASCE Journals, NCHRP panels and the Florida Hurricane Loss Commission.
